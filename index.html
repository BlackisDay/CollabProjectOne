MyFTS

Welcome to MyFTS! If you have stumbled across this page you must be wondering. What is MyFTS? Well to begin with. MyFTS is a website that lets you keep track of YOUR Favorite Team Stats. What does FTS stand for? Well FTS stands for "Favorite Team Stats". We all have a favorite team, whether it be in Basketball,Baseball, or Football. Currently MyFTS helps you keep track of your Favorite Team Stats making it easier for everyone to see.

About Us

What's worse than getting drenched while at a football game? Well with our new innovative website, not only can you see when your team plays, thanks to MyFTS (My Favorite Team Stats), you can also see the weather conditions in the area. This is especially useful for fans who attend games frequently, or are season pass holders. MyFTS also showcases last season's standings so that you can keep track of the progress your team has made.
